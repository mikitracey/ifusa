Join Ice and Fire USA

Ice and Fire USA has two types of membership, Associate and Instructor. The associate membership is for most members, especially those with instructors already in their area. The Instructor membership is designed specifically for instructors.

Benefits of Associate Membership

  • Members discounts to courses offered by Ice and Fire USA and Ice and Fire UK.
  • Twice yearly, member supported, Web of Wyrd magazine delivered by pdf.
  • I&F Training log and syllabus.
  • Member email list
  • Members who are not certified instructors may still get a website for their club for a small fee

Benefits of Instructor Membership

  • Members discounts to courses offered by Ice and Fire USA and Ice and Fire UK.
  • Twice yearly, member supported, Web of Wyrd magazine delivered by pdf.
  • I&F Training log and syllabus.
  • Member email list
  • 1 yr insurance coverage under our umbrella plan
  • A website for your group
