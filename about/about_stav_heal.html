What is Stáv

Stav Healing Principles

As with all holisitic Mind-Body-Spirit systems, Stav has a tradition for healing the body, the mind and the spirit. This tradition incorporates the five principles and the philosophy of Galder/Seid.

Trell: Assistant to healer

Karl:
    Galder: Herbalism
    Seid: Shamanic journeying

Herse:
    Galder: Joint/muscle manipulation
    Seid: Energy transfer

Jarl:
    Galder: Runic counselling
    Seid: Rune magic

Konge: Any or all of the above
