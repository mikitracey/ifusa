Connections Reloaded :: Ajay D'Souza


 

This is version 1.5 of the 'Connections Reloaded' theme.

Please follow the steps in the sidebar of this page, but if you would need more assistance please visit the theme page or preferably the Support Forums.

As the name suggests, Connections Reloaded is a revamped version of the highly popular and extremely beautiful Connections theme by Patricia Müller.

If you like the theme, please do consider a donation. It helps defray the expenses of maintaining and developing the theme and my site. Maybe a quick look through " Why should you Donate?" will motivate you to do so.

Thanks.

Ajay D'Souza

 

  • Step 1:

    • Unzip the contents of the zip.

  • Step 2:

    • Copy the whole connections-reloaded folder into your wordpress's wp-content/themes/ folder

  • Step 3:

    • Go to the Presentation area of your WordPress Admin and Select the 'Connections Reloaded'

  • Step 4:

    • Goto Write > Pages
    • Create pages for About, Archives, Links and Contact.

  • Step 5:

    • Thats all !!!
    • Go Ahead and Click on View Site at the top!!!
